about me


I was born at 1960 in Moldova which back than was a part of the Soviet Union in a city called Bendery, a small city near kishinev.
As a kid we had to go to a musical school in the evenings, so I chose to learn to play the piano (it sounds better than it relly is, I dont really like it so much).
I have studied in the university of Saratov, my first and second degree I did in computer science and math.

In Saratov is where I met my husband (Roman), he studied there in the medical school (a little bit disgusting if you ask me).
We came to Israel at 1990, around the time the Soviet Union broke apart.
We live now in Ashdod. I'm working now in an insurance company called Hahshara Bituah as a programmist, and my husband is a pediatrician in Meuhedet.

I really love reading books, especially detective books and also I like going to the theater , one of my favorite plays is phantom opera
I saw it in London almost 20 years ago, and again 7 years ago.
My hero and the special character in my life was my mom, she was a math teacher in Moldova, that's why I went to learn math, she also
convinced me to learn computer science in order to have a plan b.
